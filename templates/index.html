Welcome

An application that calculates and displays various statistics for ESPN Fantasy Basketball leagues. A similar website for Yahoo leagues is here. The code/application works for (public) leagues of any size that use H2H Most Cat. scoring. For more information and to see the source code of the project, checkout the GitHub repository.

NOTE: Running the project locally from the more-features branch will give you access to more features that are not included on the website. One such feature is the ability to determine your Strength of Schedule.

TIP: You can check if your league is public by signing out of your ESPN account or going Incognito and trying to access your league office page.
If your league is private, ask your League Manager to make it public in the League Settings so that you guys can use this tool! If your league is private, my application has no way to access your league data.

If something is broken or you have feedback to share, please e-mail me at warrencrasta@gmail.com. I love hearing positive feedback!
